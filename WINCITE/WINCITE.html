WINCITE

The WINCITE is a Premier B-Plan Competition hosted by of BITS Pilani, K. K. Birla Goa Campus as we believe that B-plan competitions prove to be great catalysts in carrying the crude ideas to a level where they can be transformed into great start-ups.

we will be organising this B-plan in last week of march in BANGALORE next year i.e. 2018. The WINCITE encompassing the three rounds as ROUND ONE, ROUND TWO and FINALE . For more details download the brochure mentioned below.

DOWNLOAD BROCHURE



ROUND ONE

Participants will be asked to enter the basic details of their Idea/prototype/startup. Entries will be shortlisted with the help of our partners on the basis of the merit of entries made by the participant.

ROUND TWO

Mentors will be allotted to all shortlisted participants of round 1. Mentoring period will be of about 1 month. By the end of mentoring session, participants will have to submit their final B-Plan. Finalists will be announced based on this B-Plan.

FINALE

All the finalists will be invited to the finale of WINCITE where they will be given opportunity to pitch their B-Plan in front of a judge panel. Winner and runner-up will be declared based on this presentation
