WHAT WE DO ?

"THE BEST WAY TO PREDICT THE FUTURE IS TO CREATE IT"


Keeping this in mind, CEL, Bits Goa, aims at cultivating the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst the youth of this nation and making them aware of their potentials. CEL believes that if you can think it, you can make it, you just need to work a way out, and that is where we come into the picture. With insightful events like Business Model Canvas, B- plan competitions like Wincite, Breakaway, we help build an atmosphere, which lets you critically analyse your ideas and grow as a leader.




VISION

CEL BITS Pilani Goa has a lot of vision for the present and the future. We want to take the culture of Startups and Entrepreneurship forward by passing it on to the younger generation and helping them in their conquests for the same. We conduct a few programs to achieve these goals.


WINCITE
Wincite is a B-plan competition that will be organized in Bangalore in end march of 2017. A lot of teams from all over India will present their ideas and prototypes in front of a panel of judges. The participants will be provided with mentorships to nurture them and help them build on their ideas. Also, the winner of the competition will be provided with funding from our affiliated VCs present in the event.


STARTUP SATURDAY
We want to encourage people to think about being a part of a start-up or making one for themselves and we think there's no better way to make it happen than meeting people who have been part of the experience. Thus, we organize Startup Saturdays in which people who've been a part of the making and growth of a start-up to come and interact with the people in our college and to teach them how things in the real world work.


BREAKAWAY
We believe that the young people of our country have a lot of potential. Hence we organize a competition known as Breakaway in our college for the freshers. The aim of the competition is to provide guidance and mentorship to the people with the ideas and the potential to create something big. We help them build on their ideas and clear the obstacles that may come their way.
